Psychoterapia

Therapy comes from psychoanalysis. It usually requires a lot of time - four to five hours a week. This way you can get information about the basics, triggers, and patient needs. Two types of conversation therapy that have the best results in the treatment of depression are:

  • behavioral therapy

    Behavioral therapy is a form of psychodynamic therapy - based on emotional and mental responses to external events, in the present and in childhood.

  • interpersonal therapy

    Interpersonal therapy focuses on direct everyday life.

Pharmacotherapy

Medication is the fundamental treatment for depression. Four classes of antidepressants are currently available. The most popular are SSRIs that cause higher levels of serotonin in the brain. Antidepressants can only be prescribed by a doctor who chooses the medicine and dose. The duration of treatment is determined only by the doctor.

Self-medication can harm health.
