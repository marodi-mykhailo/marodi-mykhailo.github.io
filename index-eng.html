What is depression?

Depression: more serious than we thought

Do you have a depressed mood? Does everything happen wrong? Your appetite has dropped? Is your sleep interrupted? Is it hard to think and concentrate? - think about it, these may be the first signs of depression.

What is it in reality:
A group of psychiatric disorders, a term used in psychiatric terminology and referring to depressive syndromes occurring in the course of affective disorders (also called affective disorders or mood disorders).
